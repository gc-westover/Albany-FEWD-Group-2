Our Weekly Needs

We are currently serving approximately 75 friends a week. We would not be able to make a difference without the support of our community and wonderful people like you!

Individual Lunch Bags (Need: 25)

Suggestions include:

  • sandwich
  • piece of fruit
  • cookie pack
  • chips
  • drink
  • cheese stick
  • cheese & crackers
  • beef jerky
  • granola bar
  • protein bar
Meal Bags for 4-6 People (Need: 75)

Some meal ideas:

  • box of pasta, jar of sauce, package of sausage or hamburger & Italian bread
  • chicken, box of stuffing, instant mashed potatoes, can of corn or green beans, cranberry sauce
  • hamburger, can of sloppy goe, hamburger rolls, can of veggies
  • hot dogs, hot dog rolls, can of beans, can of corn
  • package of American cheese, loaf of bread, stick of butter, cans of tomato soup
  • bagels with cream cheese, dozen eggs, orange juice
  • gallon of milk, box of cereal, box of Pop Tarts/muffins/donuts
