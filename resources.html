How You Can Make a Difference

Time

You do not need to be a superhero to make a difference. During this time, we are in contanst need of compassion, kindness, patience, and a whole lot of laughter - an extra hand or two wouldn't hurt either! If you're looking to volunteer with Street Soldiers Troy, simply come to Baker Park for setup at 6:30pm. We kick off festivities at 7pm, every Thursday evening, rain or shine.

Looking for some more information? You can contact Robin Ford at streetsoldierstroy@gmail.com or chat with one of our many volunteers at the office, simply call (518) 265-7026.

Shopping

Schedule conflicts but want to contribute? Street Soldiers || Troy has both an Amazon wishlist AND a PayPal Account. Items from our wishlist are used every week to assist our friends! Monetary funds from PayPal allow us to buy buspasses, groceries, and anything else that may be needed!

We are absolutely honored to be the benefactor of the profits made from the designer himself, Richard Ruotolo, "The New Norm" t-shirt! This truly is a gift! Each dollor above cost (which is minimal) goes 100% to Street Soldiers || Troy!! We were on Channel 13 News. In case you missed it, the link is below to purchase a shirt or send one to your family afar while supporting your community.

Rich is a volunteer with our organization and sees the needs, sees the gratitude, and sees how we are making a difference. We are thrilled for this opportunity!
