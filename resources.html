How You Can Make a Difference

Time

You do not need to be a superhero to make a difference. During this time, we are in constant need of compassion, kindness, patience, and a whole lot of laughter - an extra hand or two wouldn't hurt either! If you're looking to volunteer with Street Soldiers II Troy, simply come to Barker Park for setup at 6:30pm. We kick off festivities at 7pm, every Thursday evening, rain or shine.

Looking for some more information? You can contact Robin Ford at streetsoldierstroy@gmail.com or chat with one of our many volunteers at the office, simply call (518) 265-7026.

Shopping

Schedule conflicts but want to contribute? Street Soldiers II Troy has both an Amazon wishlist AND a PayPal Account. Items from our wishlist are used every week to assist our friends! Monetary funds from PayPal allow us to buy bus passes, groceries, and anything else that may be needed!

We are absolutely honored to be the benefactor of the profits made from the designer himself, Richard Ruotolo, "The New Norm" t-shirt! This truly is a gift! Each dollor above cost (which is minimal) goes 100% to Street Soldiers II Troy!! We were on Channel 13 News. In case you missed it, the link is below to purchase a shirt or send one to your family afar while supporting your community.

Rich is a volunteer with our organization and sees the needs, sees the gratitude, and sees how we are making a difference. We are thrilled for this opportunity!

Our Weekly Needs

We are currently serving approximately 75 friends a week. We would not be able to make a difference without the support of our community and wonderful people like you!

Individual Lunch Bags (Need: 25)

Suggestions include:

  • sandwich
  • piece of fruit
  • cookie pack
  • chips
  • drink
  • cheese stick
  • cheese & crackers
  • beef jerky
  • granola bar
  • protein bar

Meal Bags for 4-6 People (Need: 75)

Some meal ideas:

  • box of pasta, jar of sauce, package of sausage or hamburger & Italian bread
  • chicken, box of stuffing, instant mashed potatoes, can of corn or green beans, cranberry sauce
  • hamburger, can of sloppy joe, hamburger rolls, can of veggies
  • hot dogs, hot dog rolls, can of beans, can of corn
  • package of American cheese, loaf of bread, stick of butter, cans of tomato soup
  • bagels with cream cheese, dozen eggs, orange juice
  • gallon of milk, box of cereal, box of Pop Tarts/muffins/donuts

Purchase from our Amazon Wishlist

If you are not able to donate directly to our weekly food distribution efforts, please consider purchasing items from our Amazon Wishlist.
